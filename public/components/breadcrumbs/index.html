Breadcrumbs

Last Updated
2021-08-26T01:20:18.943Z

Breadcrumbs are a navigational pattern that show users their current location relative to the information architecture and enable users to quickly move up to a parent level. They are effective in experiences that have a large amount of content organized in a hierarchy of more than two levels.
